~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

#. SSH into the BIG-IP and enter traffic manangement shell by typing **tmsh**

#. Enter the following commands

   .. code-block:: console

      modify auth radius-server system_auth_name1 secret secret
